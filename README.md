# retail-sales-data-analysis
Welcome to the **Vrinda Store Data Analysis Project** repository!

Comprehensive retail data analysis in Excel — from raw data to actionable insights.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Project Overview

This project analyzes Vrinda Store’s retail sales data (2022) using Excel.
It demonstrates data cleaning, processing, visualization, and insights with PivotTables, charts, and dashboards.

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Key Bussiness Questions Answered

- Identify the month with highest sales and orders
- Analyze customer demographics
- Explored different order statuses in 2022
- Top contributing states by Sales
- Relationship between age and gender based on number of orders
- Highlight the highest selling category
- Which channel contributes maximum sales

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Insights

- 📈 March recorded the highest sales
- 👩 Women shop more — their share is 64%
- ✅ 92% of orders were successfully delivered
- 🌍 Top contributing states: Maharashtra, Karnataka, Uttar Pradesh, Telangana, Tamil        Nadu
- 👩 Middle-aged women (30–49 years) shop the most (34.59%)
- 👩 Young adult women (18–29 years) follow with (21.13%)
- 🛒 Sales channels: Amazon (36%), Myntra (23%), Flipkart (22%)


-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Business Recommendations

- **Seasonal Strategy** - Since March has the highest sales, Vrinda Store should plan seasonal promotions around this month.
- **Age-Specific Marketing** -
  - For middle-aged women (30–49 years): emphasize comfort, durability, and family-         oriented bundles in Kurta & Sets.
  - For young adult women (18–29 years): highlight trendy, stylish sets with influencer-driven campaigns.
  - For men: Create gift bundles (Western dresses + accessories) to encourage higher-value purchases. Since men buy more Western dresses and set for women around August.
- **Maintain Delivery Efficiency** - With 92% of orders delivered successfully, Vrinda Store should continue strengthening logistics.
- **Focus on Top States** - Regional campaigns, localized offers, and better distribution in these states can further boost sales.
- **Channel Optimization** - Since Amazon (36%) drives maximum sales, Vrinda Store should strengthen its partnership with Amazon, while also investing in growth strategies for Myntra and Flipkart to balance channel dependency
- **Expand Kurta & Set Collections** - Since Kurta and Sets are the top products for women (both middle-aged and young adults), Vrinda Store should introduce more designs, seasonal collections, and bundle offers in this category.
- **Reduce Focus on Bottom Wear** - Bottoms are the least purchased across all age groups, so inventory should be optimized.

## Business Impact

This analysis provides Vrinda Store with a clear understanding of customer behavior, product performance, and sales channels.
By focusing on women shoppers (64% share), strengthening Kurta & Set collections, optimizing inventory in top states, and leveraging Amazon as the leading channel, Vrinda Store can increase sales efficiency and customer satisfaction.
